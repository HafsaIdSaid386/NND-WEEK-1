4. Key Findings & Conclusion

Based on the exploratory data analysis of the Titanic dataset, gender and passenger class were the most important factors affecting survival. Female passengers showed a significantly higher survival rate compared to male passengers.

Passenger class also played a major role, with first-class passengers having much higher survival chances than those in third class. Age had a moderate impact, with children generally surviving more often than adults.
